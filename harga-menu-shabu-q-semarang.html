Harga Menu Shabu Q Semarang

Admin December 8, 2024

Introduction

Harga Menu Shabu Q Semarang -

Harga Menu Shabu Q Semarang - Shabu-Q, a Japanese-inspired hot pot experience, has gained immense popularity in Indonesia, particularly in Semarang. With its unique concept of yakiniku (Japanese BBQ) and shabu (hot pot), Shabu-Q offers an assortment of mouth-watering dishes to satisfy your cravings. In this article, we will delve into the world of Shabu-Q Semarang's menu, highlighting the various options available to suit every taste and budget.

Types of Menu

Shabu-Q Semarang provides an extensive menu, categorized into several sections to cater to different preferences. Here are some of the main types of menu to expect:

Appetizers

Before diving into the main course, why not start with some appetizers? Shabu-Q offers a selection of Japanese-inspired appetizers, such as:

  • Edamame: steamed soybeans in their pods
  • Gyoza: pan-fried dumplings filled with pork and vegetables
  • Agedashi tofu: deep-fried tofu in a savory broth
  • California mix: a mix of lettuce, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and avocado

Prices range from IDR 25,000 to IDR 50,000(approximately USD 1.80 to USD 3.60).

Shabu-Q Dishes

This is the main event! Shabu-Q offers a variety of dishes for you to cook and enjoy:

  • Shabu-Q Premium: a selection of high-quality meats, including wagyu beef, pork belly, and chicken breast
  • Shabu-Q Classic: a mix of meats, including pork, chicken, and beef
  • Vegetarian: a plant-based option featuring tofu, vegetables, and mushrooms

Prices range from IDR 100,000 to IDR 250,000(approximately USD 7.20 to USD 18).

Yakiniku (Japanese BBQ) Options

For those who prefer a more traditional Japanese-style BBQ, Shabu-Q offers an assortment of yakiniku options:

  • Yakiniku beef: thinly sliced wagyu beef cooked at your table
  • Yakiniku pork: pork belly and ribs cooked to perfection
  • Yakiniku chicken: marinated chicken breast cooked on a hot grill

Prices range from IDR 75,000 to IDR 200,000(approximately USD 5.40 to USD 14.40).

Desserts

Finish your meal with a sweet treat from Shabu-Q's dessert menu:

  • Mochi ice cream: Japanese-style ice cream wrapped in glutinous rice flour
  • Green tea ice cream: a refreshing and creamy dessert
  • Tofu fa: a sweet and silky tofu dessert

Prices range from IDR 20,000 to IDR 30,000(approximately USD 1.40 to USD 2.10).

Special Deals and Promotions

Shabu-Q Semarang regularly offers special deals and promotions to enhance your dining experience:

  • Happy hour: enjoy discounted drinks and appetizers during certain hours of the day
  • Loyalty program: accumulate points to redeem free dishes and services
  • Themed events: join special themed events, such as Karaoke Nights and BBQ Parties

Conclusion

Shabu-Q Semarang's menu is a treasure trove of flavors and varieties, catering to different tastes and budgets. From appetizers to desserts, and from shabu to yakiniku, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Whether you're a foodie or just looking for a unique dining experience, Shabu-Q Semarang is definitely worth a visit. So, what are you waiting for? Book your table now and indulge in the world of Shabu-Q!
