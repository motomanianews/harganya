Sejarah Mod Tarot Mini

Mod tarot, which stands for"modern tarot," is a style of tarot card reading that emerged in the 20th century. It combines traditional tarot symbolism with modern artistic styles and designs.Mod tarot mini, in particular, is a smaller version of the traditional mod tarot deck, measuring around 2.5 inches by 3.5 inches. This compact size makes it easy to carry around and convenient for readings on-the-go.

Fitur Mod Tarot Mini

Mod tarot mini decks typically feature 78 cards, including 22 major arcana cards and 56 minor arcana cards. The cards are designed to be visually striking, with bold illustrations and colorful artwork. The deck may also feature a guidebook or instruction manual to help you learn how to read the cards.
