Harga L300 Solar Klaten Thn 97

Harga L300 Solar Klaten Thn 97 - Say Hi to Skoda-Lada L300, the small yet mighty pickup truck that has carved out a niche for itself in the Indonesian automotive market. In this article, we'll delve into the details of the L300 Solar Klaten, specifically focusing on its price and specifications back in 1997.

Sejarah Singkat

The Skoda-Lada L300 is a rugged and reliable pickup truck that has been in production since the 1970s. With its narrow body and compact design, the L300 is perfect for navigating Indonesia's congested city streets and rural roads. The L300 Solar, in particular, is a high-performance variant that features a more powerful engine and improved suspension.

Spesifikasi L300 Solar Klaten Tahun 1997

When the L300 Solar Klaten was first introduced in 1997, it boasted impressive specifications that set it apart from its competitors. Here are some of its key features:

  • Engine: The L300 Solar Klaten was powered by a 2.3-liter inline-four diesel engine, producing 65 horsepower at 4,000 rpm and 148 Nm of torque at 2,000 rpm.
  • Transmission: The truck featured a 5-speed manual transmission, allowing drivers to tackle rough terrain with ease.
  • Suspension: The L300 Solar Klaten boasted a leaf-spring rear suspension and a coil-spring front suspension, providing a comfortable ride and improved handling.
  • Brakes: The truck was equipped with hydraulic drum brakes on all four wheels, ensuring safe and reliable stopping power.
  • Dimensions: The L300 Solar Klaten measured 4,355 mm in length, 1,730 mm in width, and 1,840 mm in height, with a wheelbase of 2,500 mm and a ground clearance of 190 mm.

Harga L300 Solar Klaten Tahun 1997

So, what was the price of the L300 Solar Klaten in 1997? According to various sources, the truck was priced at around IDR 120 million to IDR 140 million, depending on the specifications and trim level.

Fungsi dan Kepemuan

The L300 Solar Klaten is a versatile pickup truck that's perfect for both personal and commercial use. Its rugged design and powerful engine make it an ideal choice for off-road adventures, while its compact size and fuel-efficient engine make it suitable for city driving. With its impressive load capacity and towing capabilities, the L300 Solar Klaten is also a popular choice among small business owners and entrepreneurs.

Kesimpulan

In conclusion, the Skoda-Lada L300 Solar Klaten is a reliable and capable pickup truck that has stood the test of time. Its impressive specifications, rugged design, and affordable price make it an attractive choice for many Indonesian car buyers. Whether you're looking for a reliable workhorse or a fun off-road companion, the L300 Solar Klaten is definitely worth considering.
