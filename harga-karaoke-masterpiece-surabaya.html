Karaoke Masterpiece Surabaya: A Popular Choice

Masterpiece is a well-known karaoke brand in Indonesia, offering a wide range of machines and services to suit various needs and budgets. In Surabaya, Masterpiece has multiple branches that offer karaoke rentals, party packages, and other services. Here's a brief overview of what you can expect from Masterpiece Surabaya:

Karaoke Machines

Masterpiece Surabaya offers a variety of karaoke machines to suit different group sizes and preferences. These machines feature a vast song database, built-in microphones, and easy-to-use controls. You can choose from different models, including the Masterpiece PRO, Masterpiece Ultimate, and Masterpiece VIP.

Karaoke Rentals

If you're planning a one-off event or party, renting a karaoke machine from Masterpiece Surabaya might be the best option. They offer daily rentals, starting from IDR 500,000 to IDR 2,000,000, depending on the machine and package chosen. This includes delivery, setup, and pickup services.

Party Packages

Masterpiece Surabaya also offers party packages that cater to different event themes and sizes. Packages typically include the karaoke machine, microphones, speaker systems, and a team of dedicated event staff. Prices vary depending on the package and duration, ranging from IDR 3,000,000 to IDR 10,000,000.

Here's a breakdown of the prices you can expect to pay for karaoke Masterpiece in Surabaya:

Rentals

  • Masterpiece Ultimate (5 hours): IDR 750,000
  • Masterpiece VIP (5 hours): IDR 1,000,000
  • Daily rental (without delivery): IDR 200,000

Party Packages

  • Basic Package (2 hours): IDR 3,000,000
  • Premium Package (3 hours): IDR 5,000,000
  • Deluxe Package (5 hours): IDR 8,000,000
  • VIP Package (8 hours): IDR 10,000,000

Keep in mind that prices may vary depending on the branch location, machine availability, and other factors.

Tips for Choosing the Right Karaoke Package

When selecting a karaoke package, consider the following factors:

  • Group size: Make sure the package can accommodate your guest list.
  • Event theme: Choose a package that fits your event's style and atmosphere.
  • Budget: Set a budget and look for packages that fit within it.
  • Add-ons: Consider additional services like professional emcees, sound systems, and lighting.

By considering these factors, you'll be able to find the perfect karaoke package for your Surabaya event or party.

Hiring Professional Emcees

If you want to elevate your karaoke experience, consider hiring professional emcees from Masterpiece Surabaya. They'll help keep the party going, read out song lyrics, and ensure a smooth flow of activities.

Additional Services

Masterpiece Surabaya also offers additional services, such as:

  • Lighting and sound systems for a more immersive experience
  • Customized karaoke themes and backdrops
  • Professional event staff for setup and teardown

You can customize your package to fit your specific needs and budget.

Conclusion

Masterpiece Surabaya offers a range of karaoke options for events and parties, from rentals to party packages. With their high-quality machines, experienced staff, and flexible pricing, Masterpiece is an excellent choice for those looking for a memorable karaoke experience in Surabaya.
