What is Fuwa Fuwa Cake?

Fuwa Fuwa Cake is a type of Japanese-inspired sponge cake that is known for its light and airy texture. The cake is made with a special technique that involves incorporating air into the batter, resulting in a cake that is both tender and moist. In Indonesia, Fuwa Fuwa Cake has become a popular treat among cake lovers, and Surabaya is no exception.
