Harga Motor Honda Air Blade 125 - Motor Honda Air Blade 125 adalah salah satu varian motor sport kelas bawah dari Honda yang diluncurkan di Indonesia. Spesifikasi Motor Honda Air Blade 125

  • Engine: 124,6cc, 1-cylinder, 4-stroke, SOHC
  • Power: 9,7 kW (13,1 HP) at 9.000 rpm
  • Torque: 10,4 Nm at 7.000 rpm
  • Transmission: 5-speed manual
  • Suspension: Telescopic fork (front) and swingarm (rear)
  • Brake: Drum brake (front) and disc brake (rear)
  • Wheelbase: 1.280 mm
  • Outer diameter: 17 inches

Fitur Motor Honda Air Blade 125

  • Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS)
  • Combined Braking System (CBS)
  • Digital Gauge Cluster
  • Sporty Seat
  • Step-Up Seat
  • Adjustable Rear Suspension
  • Kick Starter
  • Side Stand

Kelebihan dan Kekurangan Motor Honda Air Blade 125
