Harga Fiat 500 Nuova 1960 - The Fiat 500, also known as the Nuova 500, is a small passenger car produced by Italians automaker Fiat. The vehicle was first introduced in 1957 and was designed to be a symbol of Italian industry and design. The Nuova 500 is a significant model in the history of Fiat, with more than 4.3 million units produced during its production run. In this article, we will explore the history of the Fiat 500 Nuova 1960, its specifications, and its owners.

Sejarah Fiat 500 Nuova 1960

The Fiat 500 Nuova 1960 was released in 1957, replacing the original Fiat 500"Topolino". The new model was designed by Dante Giacosa, a renowned Italian engineer and designer. The Nuova 500 was more modern and comfortable than its predecessor, with a number of innovative features, such as an electric starter and a monocoque body.

The model was an instant success, both in Italy and abroad. Its popularity can be attributed to its affordability, fuel efficiency, and stylish design. The Fiat 500 Nuova 1960 was available in several trim levels, including the basic"L", the mid-range"SL", and the more luxurious"SS".

Spesifikasi Fiat 500 Nuova 1960

The Fiat 500 Nuova 1960 was powered by a 500cc four-stroke engine, producing 23 horsepower. The engine was mated to a four-speed manual transmission, with a top speed of approximately 100 km/h. The vehicle weighed around 650 kg and had a length of 3,200 mm.

The Nuova 500 was available in several body styles, including a four-seat sedan, a two-seat convertible, and a three-seat station wagon. The interior was simple, but functional, with a focus on comfort and practicality.

Pemilik Fiat 500 Nuova 1960

The Fiat 500 Nuova 1960 was a popular model among both private owners and public transportation operators. In Italy, the Nuova 500 was widely used as a family car, while in other countries, it was often used as a taxi or delivery vehicle.

Today, the Fiat 500 Nuova 1960 is a sought-after classic car, with many owners and enthusiasts restoring and maintaining the vehicles. There is also a strong enthusiast community, with many events and meetups organized throughout the year.

Harga Fiat 500 Nuova 1960

  • Fiat 500 Nuova 1960 restored: IDR 150-250 million (USD 10,500-18,000)
  • Fiat 500 Nuova 1960 original restoration: IDR 100-200 million (USD 7,000-14,000)
  • Fiat 500 Nuova 1960 for restoration: IDR 50-150 million (USD 3,500-10,500)

Conclusion

The Fiat 500 Nuova 1960 is a significant model in the history of Fiat, known for its innovative design, affordability, and fuel efficiency. Today, the model is a sought-after classic car, with many owners and enthusiasts restoring and maintaining the vehicles. Whether you're looking for a classic car to restore or want to own a piece of history, the Fiat 500 Nuova 1960 is definitely worth considering.
