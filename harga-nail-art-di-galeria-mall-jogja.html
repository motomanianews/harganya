What is Nail Art?

Nail art is a form of creative expression that involves decorating one's fingernails with various designs, patterns, and colors. It has become a popular trend in the beauty industry, as it allows individuals to showcase their unique style and personality. Nail art can be simple, with basic designs like stripes or dots, or intricate, with complex patterns and characters.
