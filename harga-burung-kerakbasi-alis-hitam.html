But what makes the burung kerakbasi alis hitam so valuable, and what is its average price range? What Makes the Burung Kerakbasi Alis Hitam So Valuable?

The burung kerakbasi alis hitam is undoubtedly one of the most stunning bird species in the world. Its sleek black cap, white cheek patch, and gray-white body make it a true masterpiece of nature. But what makes it so valuable? Several factors contribute to its high demand:

  1. Rarity: The burung kerakbasi alis hitam is endemic to a specific region of Southeast Asia, making it a rare and sought-after species among bird enthusiasts.
  2. Coloration: Its striking black and white Plumage is unlike any other bird species, making it a prized possession among collectors.
  3. Behavior: The burung kerakbasi alis hitam is known for its bold and fearless behavior, making it a joy to observe in its natural habitat.

Average Price Range of Burung Kerakbasi Alis Hitam

The average price range of the burung kerakbasi alis hitam varies depending on several factors, including its age, sex, and condition. Here are some general price ranges:

  1. Captive-bred juveniles: RS 500,000 - RS 1,000,000 (approximately USD 33,000 - USD 66,000)
  2. Captive-bred adults: RS 2,000,000 - RS 5,000,000 (approximately USD 133,000 - USD 333,000)
  3. Wild-caught birds: RS 5,000,000 - RS 10,000,000 (approximately USD 333,000 - USD 666,000)

Things to Consider Before Buying a Burung Kerakbasi Alis Hitam

Before buying a burung kerakbasi alis hitam, there are several things to consider:

  1. Licensing: Check with local authorities to ensure you have the necessary permits and licenses to own an exotic bird.
  2. Housing: Ensure that your home has a suitable environment for the bird, including a large cage or aviary and adequate ventilation.
  3. Diet: The burung kerakbasi alis hitam requires a specialized diet, including a variety of seeds, fruits, and insects.
  4. Care and Handling: The burung kerakbasi alis hitam requires regular handling and socialization to maintain its health and well-being.

Conclusion

The burung kerakbasi alis hitam is a truly unique and captivating bird species that requires special care and attention. Its striking appearance and impressive flying skills make it a prized possession among bird enthusiasts. With its average price range varying from RS 500,000 to RS 10,000,000, the burung kerakbasi alis hitam is a significant investment for anyone looking to own this exotic bird. By considering the factors mentioned above, you can ensure a happy and healthy relationship with your new feathered friend.
