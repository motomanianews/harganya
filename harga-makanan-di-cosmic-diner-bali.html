Harga Makanan Di Cosmic Diner Bali

Admin December 3, 2024

Harga Makanan Di Cosmic Diner Bali - Cosmic Diner Bali is a popular restaurant in Bali, known for its unique theme and delicious dishes. Located in the heart of Kuta, Cosmic Diner Bali offers a wide range of menu options to suit every taste and budget. In this article, we will explore the prices of the menu items at Cosmic Diner Bali and provide you with a comprehensive guide to help you plan your dining experience.

Breakfast Menu

Cosmic Diner Bali offers a variety of breakfast options, ranging from classic dishes to more unique creations. Here are some of the breakfast menu items and their prices:

  • Cosmic Breakfast (IDR 85,000): A hearty breakfast dish consisting of scrambled eggs, bacon, sausages, and toast.
  • Avocado Toast (IDR 60,000): Toast topped with mashed avocado, cherry tomatoes, and feta cheese.
  • French Toast (IDR 50,000): Thick slices of bread dipped in egg and milk, grilled to perfection, and served with maple syrup.
  • Breakfast Tacos (IDR 70,000): A dish consisting of scrambled eggs, chorizo, avocado, and salsa, wrapped in a tortilla.

Lunch Menu

Cosmic Diner Bali's lunch menu offers a range of sandwiches, salads, and international dishes. Here are some of the lunch menu items and their prices:

  • Grilled Chicken Sandwich (IDR 120,000): A juicy grilled chicken breast sandwich served with lettuce, tomato, and mayo.
  • Cosmic Burger (IDR 150,000): A classic burger topped with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and Cosmic Diner's signature sauce.
  • Greek Salad (IDR 100,000): A refreshing salad consisting of mixed greens, feta cheese, olives, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  • Pad Thai (IDR 90,000): A popular Thai dish made with stir-fried rice noodles, vegetables, and your choice of protein.

Dinner Menu

Cosmic Diner Bali's dinner menu offers a range of international dishes, including Asian fusion, Italian, and American favorites. Here are some of the dinner menu items and their prices:

  • Rack of Ribs (IDR 350,000): A tender and juicy rack of ribs served with coleslaw and BBQ sauce.
  • Cosmic Pasta (IDR 200,000): A dish consisting of mixed pasta, vegetables, and your choice of protein, served in a creamy sauce.
  • Grilled Fish (IDR 250,000): A fresh catch of the day grilled to perfection and served with a side of herbs and lemon.
  • Korean BBQ (IDR 220,000): A popular Korean dish made with marinated beef, vegetables, and spicy sauce.

Desserts

Cosmic Diner Bali offers a range of delicious desserts to round off your meal. Here are some of the dessert options and their prices:

  • Cheesecake (IDR 60,000): A creamy and rich cheesecake served with a side of mixed berries.
  • Brownie (IDR 50,000): A fudgy and chocolatey brownie served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  • Tiramisu (IDR 70,000): A classic Italian dessert made with ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, and espresso.
  • Fruit Salad (IDR 40,000): A refreshing salad consisting of mixed fruit and a dollop of whipped cream.

Tips and Recommendations

Here are some tips and recommendations to help you plan your dining experience at Cosmic Diner Bali:

  • Arrive early to avoid long wait times, especially during peak hours.
  • Order the Cosmic Breakfast* for a filling and delicious breakfast option.
  • Try the Grilled Fish* for a fresh and flavorful dinner option.
  • Don't forget to leave room for dessert and try one of the delicious options on the menu.

Conclusion

Cosmic Diner Bali offers a range of delicious menu options at affordable prices. Whether you're looking for a hearty breakfast, a fresh and flavorful lunch, or a romantic dinner, Cosmic Diner Bali has something for everyone. With its unique theme and friendly service, it's a must-visit destination for foodies and locals alike.
