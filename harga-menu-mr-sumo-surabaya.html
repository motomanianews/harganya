Harga Menu Mr Sumo Surabaya - Harga Menu MR Sumo Surabaya: Unlock the Best Japanese Food Experience

Are you craving for authentic Japanese cuisine in Surabaya? Look no further than MR Sumo, a popular restaurant chain that serves a wide range of delicious dishes. But before you head out to taste their mouth-watering menu, let's first explore the prices of their offerings.

About MR Sumo

MR Sumo is a Japanese restaurant that was founded in Surabaya in 2013. Since then, it has become a favorite among foodies and locals alike, known for its affordable prices and generous servings. The restaurant prides itself on using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods to create a truly authentic Japanese dining experience.

Menu Price Range

MR Sumo's menu is divided into several categories, including Appetizers, Soups, Salads, and Main Courses. Here's a breakdown of their menu price range:

Appetizers

  • Edamame (boiled soybeans) - IDR 12,000 - IDR 15,000
  • Gyoza (pan-fried dumplings) - IDR 25,000 - IDR 35,000
  • Agedashi Tofu (deep-fried tofu) - IDR 25,000 - IDR 35,000

Soups

  • Miso Soup - IDR 10,000 - IDR 15,000
  • Udon Noodle Soup - IDR 20,000 - IDR 30,000
  • Ramen Soup - IDR 30,000 - IDR 50,000

Salads

  • Chicken Teriyaki Salad - IDR 35,000 - IDR 50,000
  • Salmon Sashimi Salad - IDR 50,000 - IDR 70,000
  • Veggie Salad - IDR 20,000 - IDR 30,000

Main Courses

  • Chicken Katsu (breaded and fried chicken) - IDR 50,000 - IDR 70,000
  • Salmon Sushi (6 pieces) - IDR 120,000 - IDR 150,000
  • Beef Yakiniku (grilled beef) - IDR 80,000 - IDR 120,000

Conclusion

MR Sumo's menu price range is affordable and offers a wide range of options to suit every taste and budget. From appetizers to main courses, the restaurant's dishes are all carefully crafted to provide an authentic Japanese dining experience. Whether you're a foodie or just looking for a quick bite, MR Sumo is definitely a restaurant worth trying.

Address

MR Sumo Surabaya is located at:

Jl. Raya Dukuh Sambela No. 22, Kemayoran, Surabaya 60223, Indonesia

Opening Hours

MR Sumo Surabaya is open daily from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
