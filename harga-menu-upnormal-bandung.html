Bandung, a city known for its foodie culture and unique culinary experiences. Menu and Pricing: A Culinary Delight

Upnormal Bandung offers a variety of menus that cater to different tastes and dietary needs. From traditional Indonesian dishes to contemporary fusion cuisine, the menu is designed to satisfy your cravings. Here are some of the popular items on the menu:

Appétizers

  • Fresh seafood platter (IDR 250,000 - IDR 500,000)
  • Grilled vegetable skewers (IDR 120,000 - IDR 200,000)
  • Fried won ton served with sweet chili sauce (IDR 60,000 - IDR 120,000)

Main Course

  • Grilled octopus with garlic butter and lemon (IDR 200,000 - IDR 300,000)
  • Pan-seared duck breast with cherry compote (IDR 250,000 - IDR 350,000)
  • Beef tenderloin with roasted garlic mashed potatoes (IDR 220,000 - IDR 320,000)

Sides

  • Garlic and herb roasted potatoes (IDR 50,000 - IDR 100,000)
  • Steamed broccoli with lemon butter (IDR 50,000 - IDR 100,000)
  • Fried eggplant with tomato sauce (IDR 40,000 - IDR 80,000)

Desserts

  • Chocolate lava cake (IDR 60,000 - IDR 120,000)
  • Fresh fruit salad with yogurt and granola (IDR 40,000 - IDR 80,000)
  • Tiramisu with espresso shot (IDR 50,000 - IDR 100,000)

Beverages

  • Soft drinks (IDR 20,000 - IDR 40,000)

  • Specialty coffee drinks (IDR 25,000 - IDR 50,000)

Tips and Recommendations

  • Book your table in advance, especially on weekends and holidays, to avoid long waiting times.
  • Try the signature dish, the grilled octopus, which is cooked to perfection and serves as a great appetizer.
  • Don't forget to order the chocolate lava cake for dessert, which is rich and decadent.
  • If you have a large group or celebration, consider ordering the seafood platter, which offers a variety of fresh seafood options.

Conclusion

Upnormal Bandung offers a unique dining experience that combines traditional and modern elements. With its diverse menu and reasonable pricing, it's no wonder why this restaurant is a popular choice among locals and tourists alike.
