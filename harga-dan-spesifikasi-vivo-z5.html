Vivo Z5, a flagship device from Vivo, is a high-end smartphone that offers a premium user experience. Design

Vivo Z5 boasts a sleek and modern design that exudes elegance and sophistication. The phone features a 6.67-inch AMOLED display with a stunning resolution of 1080 x 2400 pixels, making it perfect for watching videos, playing games, and browsing the web. The phone's frame is crafted from durable metal, ensuring a solid build quality and lasting durability.

Performance

Under the hood, Vivo Z5 is powered by a high-performance Qualcomm Snapdragon 888 processor, paired with up to 12GB of RAM and 256GB of internal storage. This powerful combination ensures seamless performance, whether you're gaming, multitasking, or simply using your phone for everyday tasks.

Vivo Z5 boasts a quad-camera setup, featuring a 48MP primary sensor, a 13MP ultra-wide-angle lens, a 2MP depth sensor, and a 2MP macro lens. The camera app offers a range of shooting modes, including night mode, portrait mode, and slow-motion video recording. With Vivo Z5, you can capture stunning photos and videos, whether you're a professional photographer or just a casual shutterbug.

Battery

Vivo Z5 is powered by a large 5000mAh battery, which provides all-day power and then some. With power-saving features like SuperPower Saving Mode, you can enjoy up to 2 days of battery life on a single charge.

Price

So, how much will you need to shell out to get your hands on Vivo Z5? In Indonesia, the phone is priced at around IDR 14,000,000(approximately USD 930) for the base model, which comes with 8GB of RAM and 128GB of internal storage. The higher-end model, with 12GB of RAM and 256GB of internal storage, is priced at around IDR 18,000,000(approximately USD 1,210).
