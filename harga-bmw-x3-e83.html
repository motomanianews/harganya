Spesifikasi BMW X3 E83

The BMW X3 E83 is a compact SUV that measures 4,454 mm in length, 1,796 mm in width, and 1,612 mm in height. It features a wheelbase of 2,753 mm and has a ground clearance of 172 mm. The car is powered by a range of engines, including a 2.5-liter inline-six engine producing 225 horsepower and 300 Nm of torque, as well as a 3.0-liter inline-six engine producing 231 horsepower and 315 Nm of torque.

Harga BMW X3 E83

The prices of the BMW X3 E83 vary depending on the trim level, engine, and year of manufacture. Here are some approximate prices for new and used models:

  • New BMW X3 E83 (2004-2010) : Rp 400,000,000 - Rp 700,000,000
  • Used BMW X3 E83 (2004-2010) : Rp 250,000,000 - Rp 500,000,000

  • Used BMW X3 E83 (2011-2015) : Rp 300,000,000 - Rp 700,000,000

Fitur dan Konsumsi BBM

The BMW X3 E83 is equipped with a range of features to enhance comfort and safety, including:

  • 6-cylinder engine with automatic transmission
  • 8-speed Sport automatic transmission
  • Navigation system with postcode recognition
  • Rearview camera
  • Parking sensors
  • Bi-xenon headlights with headlight washer system
  • Leather seats with electric adjustment
  • Climate control system
  • 12-speaker audio system

In terms of fuel consumption, the BMW X3 E83 has a combined fuel economy of 9.5 liters per 100 kilometers, with a city cycle fuel economy of 10.2 liters per 100 kilometers and a highway cycle fuel economy of 8.4 liters per 100 kilometers.
