Harga Pesawat Sukhoi Su 57 - The Sukhoi Su-57 is a fifth-generation stealth multirole fighter aircraft developed by Russian aerospace company Sukhoi. The aircraft has been designed to operate in extreme weather conditions, with a focus on high-altitude and high-temperature environments. In this article, we will explore the specifications, functional capabilities, and the price of the Sukhoi Su-57.

Design and Features

The Sukhoi Su-57 has a unique design, featuring a triangular-shaped fuselage and a curved fin. The aircraft is made up of composite materials, which provides improved structural strength and reduced weight. The systems onboard the Su-57 are designed to minimize radar signature, making it a highly effective stealth fighter.

One of the most impressive features of the Su-57 is its advanced avionics suite. The aircraft is equipped with a Head-Up Display(HUD), a Multi-Function Display(MFD), and a Helmet-Mounted Display(HMD). This advanced avionics suite enables the pilot to interact with the aircraft's systems in real-time.

The Sukhoi Su-57 has the following specifications:

  • Length: 17.3 meters
  • Wingspan: 14 meters
  • Height: 5.9 meters
  • Maximum takeoff weight: 26,000 kg
  • Powerplant: 2 x Saturn AL-41F1S turbofans

  • Service ceiling: 20,000 meters
  • Range: 4,000 km
  • Armament: 2 x 30mm GSh-30K cannons and 6-8 kmrons

Functional Capabilities

The Sukhoi Su-57 is a highly versatile fighter aircraft, capable of performing a wide range of missions. Some of the key functional capabilities of the Su-57 include:

  • Air-to-air combat: The Su-57 is equipped with advanced radar and tracking systems, enabling it to engage multiple targets simultaneously.
  • Air-to-ground combat: The aircraft is capable of carrying a range of weapons, including precision-guided munitions, to engage surface targets.
  • Electronic warfare: The Su-57 is equipped with advanced electronic warfare systems, enabling it to disrupt enemy communications and radar systems.
  • Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (ISR): The aircraft is capable of performing ISR missions, providing real-time intelligence on enemy forces and movements.

Price

The Sukhoi Su-57 is a highly advanced fighter aircraft, and as such, its price is not publicly disclosed. However, it is estimated that the cost of one Su-57 aircraft is around$40-50 million.

The Sukhoi Su-57 is a highly advanced stealth fighter aircraft, featuring a unique design, advanced avionics suite, and a range of functional capabilities. The aircraft is capable of performing a wide range of missions, from air-to-air combat to electronic warfare. With its estimated price tag of $40-50 million, the Su-57 is a highly impressive addition to any military fleet.
