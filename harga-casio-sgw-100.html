Introduction

Features and Specifications

Display and Design

The Casio SGW-100 features a 1.3-inch color display with a resolution of 240 x 240 pixels. It is powered by a Japanese quartz movement and has a sleek, modern design that is both stylish and durable. The smartwatch is suitable for daily wear and can withstand minor scratches and bumps.

Connectivity

The SGW-100 supports Bluetooth 4.0 connectivity, allowing you to pair it with your smartphone and receive notifications, track fitness data, and control music playback. It is also compatible with both Android and iOS devices.

Fitness Tracking

The smartwatch includes a built-in accelerometer and pedometer, allowing you to track your daily activity, including steps taken, calories burned, and distance traveled. It is also water-resistant up to 100 meters, making it perfect for swimmers and water lovers.

Other Features

  • Stopwatch and world time functions
  • Compatible with Google Fit and Apple Health

Compact Design

The smartwatch is compact and lightweight, making it easy to wear and forget about while still tracking your fitness goals.

User-Friendly Interface
