Faktor-Faktor yang Mempengaruhi Harga Rupiah Ke Ringgit

  1. Ekonomi RegionalRegional economy plays a significant role in determining the exchange rate between the Rupiah and the Ringgit. When an economy is doing well, the value of its currency increases, and vice versa. For example, when Indonesia's economy is booming, the value of the Rupiah tends to increase, making it more expensive for Malaysian businesses and individuals to import goods and services from Indonesia. Conversely, when Malaysia's economy is struggling, the value of the Ringgit decreases, making it cheaper for Indonesian businesses and individuals to import goods and services from Malaysia.
  2. Inflation RateInflation rate is another important factor influencing the exchange rate between the Rupiah and the Ringgit. When inflation is high in a country, the value of its currency tends to decrease, as foreign investors become wary of investing in a country with high inflation. On the other hand, when inflation is low, the value of the currency tends to increase.
  3. Interest RateInterest rates also play a crucial role in determining the exchange rate between the Rupiah and the Ringgit. When interest rates are high in a country, investors tend to invest in that country's assets, such as bonds and stocks, which increases the value of the currency. Conversely, when interest rates are low, investors tend to invest in other countries with higher interest rates, which decreases the value of the currency.
  4. Global Economic ConditionsGlobal economic conditions, such as global trade tensions, economic downturns, and monetary policy changes, also affect the exchange rate between the Rupiah and the Ringgit. When global economic conditions are uncertain or negative, investors tend to seek safer assets, such as US dollars or Japanese yen, which increases the value of those currencies and decreases the value of emerging market currencies, such as the Rupiah and the Ringgit.

Analisis Harga Rupiah Ke Ringgit

Based on the factors mentioned above, we can analyze the historical data of the exchange rate between the Rupiah and the Ringgit. According to the data, the Rupiah has been experiencing a stable to slightly declining trend against the Ringgit since 2018. Prediksi Harga Rupiah Ke Ringgit
