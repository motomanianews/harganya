What is Hand Sanitizer?

Before we dive into the pricing strategies, it's essential to understand what hand sanitizer is and its importance in our daily lives. Hand sanitizer is a liquid, gel, or foam that contains antibacterial ingredients, such as ethanol or isopropanol, which help to kill or inhibit the growth of microorganisms on the skin. Hand sanitizers are designed to be used in situations where soap and water are not available, making them a convenient and effective way to maintain good hygiene.
