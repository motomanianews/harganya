Fitur Tabloid Pulsa (TP)

The Tabloid Pulsa feature is a wallet within the phone that allows users to store and manage their SIM cards, phone numbers, and credit card information. It's a convenient way to make transactions and access important information on the go.

Some of the key features of the Tabloid Pulsa include:

  • Dual-SIM support: Supports two SIM cards with different numbers and capacities.
  • Mobile payment: Allows users to make transactions using their phone.
  • Convenience: Provides easy access to important information such as phone numbers, credit card information, and more.
  • Secured: Uses advanced security features to protect user data.
